Executive Summary

AKM (Amina Meme Token) is a community-driven meme token built on the Polygon blockchain, designed to combine entertainment with innovative tokenomics. Through a deflationary burn mechanism and community-centric approach, AKM aims to create sustainable value for its holders while maintaining the fun and engaging nature of meme culture.

The cryptocurrency landscape has witnessed the explosive growth of meme tokens, demonstrating the power of community-driven projects. AKM emerges as a next-generation meme token that balances entertainment value with technical innovation and sustainable tokenomics.

• Ethereum Compatibility: Full EVM compatibility

• Environmental Friendly: Proof-of-Stake consensus

• Scalability: Handles thousands of transactions per second

3.2 Burn Mechanism

AKM implements an innovative burn mechanism:

• 1% Burn Rate: On every transaction, 1% of tokens are permanently burned

• Deflationary Supply: Total supply decreases over time

• Automatic Process: Burns are handled by smart contract

• Transparent Tracking: All burns are publicly verifiable

3.3 No Transaction Tax

Unlike many meme tokens, AKM has zero transaction taxes:

• No buy tax

• No sell tax

• No transfer tax

• Only the 1% burn mechanism applies
