Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

AKM (Amina Meme Token) has prepared this Cookie Policy to explain how we use cookies on our website. This policy explains what cookies are, why we use them, and how you can manage them.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored in your browser when you visit a website. These files help the website work more effectively and provide you with a better experience.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the website:

• Session management cookies

• Security cookies

• Language preference cookies

• Wallet connection cookies

3.2 Performance Cookies

Used to analyze website performance:

• Page view statistics

• User behavior analysis

• Error reporting

• Loading times

3.3 Functionality Cookies

Used to improve user experience:

• Preference settings

• Theme selections

• Previous visit information

• Personalized content

3.4 Analytics Cookies

To analyze website traffic and usage:

• Google Analytics

• Visitor numbers

• Popular pages

• Traffic sources

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use cookies from the following third-party services:

• Google Analytics (web analysis)

• Social Media Plugins (social media integration)

• Blockchain Analytics (transaction analysis)

• CDN Services (content distribution)

5. Cookie Management

You can manage cookies in the following ways:

5.1 Browser Settings

• Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies

• Firefox: Settings > Privacy and Security

• Safari: Preferences > Privacy

• Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

5.2 Cookie Preferences

From the cookie preference center on our website:

• Accept essential cookies

• Reject optional cookies

• Update your preferences

• Reset cookie settings

6. Cookie Storage Duration

6.1 Session Cookies

Deleted when browser is closed

6.2 Persistent Cookies

• Analytics cookies: 2 years

• Functionality cookies: 6 months

• Security cookies: 30 days
